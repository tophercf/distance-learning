Conclusion


Sharing opportunities

As distance learning grows, its impact is increasingly uneven. While technical offerings -- and the opportunities that accompany them -- have expanded, not all areas of the country have seen such uniform growth. And if specializations are the new normal, then graduate students should be more representative of society at large, not just the privileged few.

Progressive and responsive

The future of online learning is one in which administrators and educators strive to create an environment where all members of society can access knowledge, to provide the maximization of benefit, and to continue to build foundations to push the existing boundaries of education and learning.
